About Us

About Us

The Sanderson High School Athletic Club is dedicated to fostering a spirit of sportsmanship, teamwork, and excellence among our student-athletes. We strive to support and enhance the athletic experience for all students, encouraging them to achieve their personal best both on and off the field. Join us in celebrating the hard work, dedication, and achievements of our athletes. Go Spartans!

Membership

Become a member of our athletic booster club for Sanderson High School. The Athletic Club provides financial support to our athletic programs. With membership, you have a say in how our financial resources are used to fund safety equipment, facilities upgrades, uniform purchases, and much more! Plus, you will get a chance to meet other families at Sanderson who share your same passion and drive to provide the best for our athletes and coaches!

Join our Club for $15.00

Concessions

Staffing our concession stands for our home sporting events is just one of the ways the Athletic Club raises money to support the many needs of our athletes. By signing up for a shift, you can also help raise money for your athlete’s team (regardless of which season your athlete plays). A portion of each season’s profits will be distributed back to the teams based on their volunteer sign-ups.

Team Parent

A Team Parent is an important part of our Sanderson Athletic Club family. They are a key contributor to each of our sports teams while providing key support to the Coaches, Families, and Athletes. We appreciate you for volunteering to be a Team Parent to your team! Click here to read more about being a Team Parent.
